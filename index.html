Mood mirror was created by Juri Gemignani during February 2023.

Idea and design inspired by Dailyio. Icons from Iconscout.

This project is open-source and accessible by everyone, who want to modify this can create a fork on the Github Repo.

Notes are saved in local storage. Clearing your browser or site cache, switching devices or browsers will result in the loss of your notes.
